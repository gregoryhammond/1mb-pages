Turretcss Framework


Developed for design, turretcss is a styles and browser behaviour normalisation framework for rapid development of responsive and accessible websites.

turretcss.com

Their website or Github repo doesn't mention anything about the size of the framework, or even if they try to make it small in size. They do mention on their homepage that their principles for this are simple, smart, and modern. Which you can read more about on turretcss.com.

You can find Turretcss at turretcss.com, Github, npm, and JsDelivr.

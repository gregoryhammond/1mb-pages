Showing What Can Be Done With 1MB


The web has become so big and large that a majority of websites don't care how big their website is, they care about it looking good. This shouldn't tbe the case, we should still be making websites small in size so anyone (no matter their internet) can use the pages.

The idea for this came from 1MB Club.

My challenge is to make a number of webpages that are 1MB or less in size. I will be creating a number of different pages in different ways.

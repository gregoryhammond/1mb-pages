Pure Framework

Pure.css was created by Yahoo, and version 0.0.1 came out on May 14th 2013.

The first public release coming out July 2nd of the same year.

The contributors have mostly been Yahoo employees (at the time they were employed by Yahoo) but there has been help by regular people.
The idea for this came from 1MB Club. My challenge is to make a number of webpages that are 1MB or less in size. I will be creating a number of different pages in different ways.

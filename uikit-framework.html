UIkit Framework


UIKit is created by the Yootheme but has many contributors who aren't employed by Yootheme. Version 1.0.0 came out on July 19th 2013, and there have been new releases almost continously since then.
Currently the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Opera are supported. As well as Safari from version 9 above, and Internet Explorer from version 11 and above.

The idea for this came from 1MB Club.

My challenge is to make a number of webpages that are 1MB or less in size. I will be creating a number of different pages in different ways.
